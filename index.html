Addictive Indie Game for iPhone & iPad

 

 

Wicked jellies have bitten off Bob's fingers when he was asleep while fishing. Help Bob in his adventure to return fingers back! You will have 60 seconds to catch as many jellies as you can in a colorful animated world full of comical effects.

Each jellyfish caught can be redeemed for Power-Ups to improve your results.
